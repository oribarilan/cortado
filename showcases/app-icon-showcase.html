Cortado App Icon

Full-color app icon variants for Cortado. Shown at multiple sizes with macOS squircle mask applied. This icon appears in Finder, Spotlight, Activity Monitor, the About dialog, and DMG.

How macOS app icons work: macOS automatically applies a squircle (superellipse) mask to app icons. The source image should be a full 1024x1024 square — the OS crops it. The icon design should be centered with some padding from the edges so nothing important gets clipped by the mask.

Sizes: The icon is used from 1024px (App Store) down to 16px (Finder list view). The design must remain legible and recognizable at all sizes — avoid fine details that disappear at 32px.
